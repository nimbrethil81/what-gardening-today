🌱 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

"What Gardening Today?" ("the app") is a small, personal gardening app. This page explains, in plain language, what information the app collects, why, and how it's used.

Who runs this app

The app is built and run by a single independent developer, not a company. If you have any question about your data, contact stingyfit@gmail.com.

What information is collected

  • Your sign-in identity. When you sign in, the app receives your email address — either directly (if you sign in by email) or from Google (if you choose "Continue with Google," which shares your Google account's name, email address, and profile picture). This is used only to recognise you as a returning user and to control who is allowed to use the app.
  • Your garden data. Anything you enter yourself: your garden's name, its approximate location (from a postcode or your device's location, used only to fetch local weather and relevant seasonal timing), the plants and structures you add, and which tasks you've completed or hidden.
  • Nothing else. The app does not collect browsing history, contacts, photos, or any data beyond what's listed above, and it does not use tracking cookies or analytics trackers.

Why this information is collected

Solely to make the app work: to know who you are, to show you your own garden and nobody else's, and to tailor the day's gardening tasks to your location's weather and to what you've actually planted.

Who this information is shared with

Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers. It is shared only with the technical services that make the app run, strictly as needed for their function:

  • Supabase — hosts the database that stores your account and garden data.
  • Google — if you choose "Continue with Google," Google handles that sign-in step; the app only receives your basic profile details in return.
  • OpenWeather — receives your garden's approximate coordinates to return the current weather; it does not receive your identity.
  • postcodes.io — used only at setup, to turn a postcode into a location; it does not receive your identity.

Access control

This app is invitation-only. New accounts cannot be created by just anyone — an email address must be added by the app's owner before it can sign in. Each person's garden data is kept private and is never visible to any other user of the app.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. If you'd like your account and all associated garden data permanently deleted, contact stingyfit@gmail.com and this will be done promptly.

Children

This app is not directed at children and is not knowingly used to collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change accordingly. As this is a small, single-developer project, changes (if any) are expected to be minor and infrequent.
